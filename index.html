Meta Glasses Setup

Voice search, wrist gestures & how to
install ViewTube on your glasses

Wrist Gestures

👆

Wrist Tilt Up

Scroll the feed upward — browse to newer content. Works on home & search results.

👇

Wrist Tilt Down

Scroll the feed downward — go back to previous content.

👈

Wrist Flick Left

Go back / close the video player. Same as the back button.

👉

Wrist Flick Right

Open Search screen. Flick right again to trigger voice search automatically.

✊

Double Wrist Shake

Activates / deactivates voice search mode. Say your query out loud.

🤙

Wrist Rotate Clockwise

Navigate tabs: Home → Search → Guide and back.

Note: Wrist gestures use your phone/glasses gyroscope (DeviceMotion API). Tap "Enable Gestures" on the Home screen to activate. On iOS you'll see a permission prompt.

Voice Search

🎤

How to use

Tap the microphone icon on the Search tab, or shake your wrist twice. Say your query clearly — results appear automatically when you stop speaking.

Tip: Voice search uses your browser's built-in speech recognition — no extra permissions needed beyond microphone access.

Install on Meta Glasses

1

Host the app online (free)

Upload the meta-youtube.html file to a free host. Easiest options: GitHub Pages, Netlify Drop (drag & drop), or Vercel. You'll get a public URL like https://yourname.github.io/viewtube

2

Open on Meta Quest / Ray-Ban Meta

On Meta Quest: open the built-in browser, navigate to your URL, then tap the ⋯ menu → "Add to Home" or pin as a panel in your space.

On Ray-Ban Meta glasses: the glasses connect to your phone. Open the URL in your phone's browser, then cast/mirror to the glasses display via the Meta View app.

3

Enable Motion Permissions

For wrist gesture support, tap "Enable Gestures" inside the app (Home screen, top right badge). On iOS, the browser will ask permission to access the gyroscope — tap Allow.

4

Enable Microphone for Voice

When you first tap the 🎤 microphone button, your browser will request mic access. Tap Allow. After that, voice search works hands-free every time.

5

Add to Home Screen (optional)

In your phone browser tap Share → Add to Home Screen. This makes ViewTube launch like a native app — full screen, no browser chrome.

Best experience: Use on Meta Quest 3 or Quest 3S browser for full immersive panel display. Hand tracking in Meta Quest natively maps to touch events — all swipe and tap gestures work out of the box with no extra setup.
